The Challenge

An author had a strong book but struggled to build a social media presence that would connect with readers, drive engagement, and establish their brand as an authority in their genre.

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive social media strategy that included:

  • ▸ Full account setup and profile optimization
  • ▸ Consistent daily posting with high-quality content
  • ▸ Strategic hashtag campaigns for maximum reach
  • ▸ Audience engagement and community building
  • ▸ Content calendar aligned with book promotions

The Result

The author's social media presence grew exponentially with 300% increase in engagement, 10,000+ new followers, and 2M+ impressions. The author became a recognized voice in their genre, leading to increased book sales and speaking opportunities.
