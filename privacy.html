Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

📅 Last Updated: January 15, 2026

📑 Table of Contents

1. Information We Collect

At Hope Holmes, we collect information to provide better services to our clients and website visitors. The types of information we collect include:

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you fill out forms or contact us.
  • Project Details: Information about your author brand, book projects, and social media goals.
  • Payment Information: Billing details when you purchase our services.
  • Communication: Records of your correspondence with us.

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device type.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, and referral sources.
  • Cookies: Small text files that enhance your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: Deliver social media management, content creation, and branding services.
  • To Communicate: Respond to inquiries, send project updates, and share relevant information.
  • To Improve: Analyze website usage to enhance user experience and service quality.
  • To Market: Send newsletters, promotions, and updates with your consent.
  • To Comply: Fulfill legal obligations and protect against fraudulent activity.

Note: We only send promotional emails with your explicit consent. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in our emails or by contacting us directly.

3. Information Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist with website hosting, email delivery, and analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our website is encrypted.
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis.
  • Secure Storage: Data stored on secure servers with firewalls and monitoring.
  • Regular Audits: Security practices reviewed and updated regularly.

Important: While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service provider.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post any changes on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
