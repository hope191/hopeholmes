About Hope Holmes

Your partner in building an author brand that stands out.

Strategic. Creative. Results-driven.

Hope Holmes is a full-service Social Media Expert specializing in helping authors, writers, entrepreneurs, coaches, and personal brands establish, grow, and manage their social media presence.

With a passion for storytelling and a keen eye for design, Hope provides complete social media solutions — from account setup and branding to content creation, engagement, and analytics.

The business provides complete social media solutions, including account setup, branding, content creation, graphic design, content scheduling, audience engagement, growth strategies, analytics tracking, and ongoing social media management.
